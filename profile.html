Our Athletes

The Kebenei Foundation Scholarship Athletic Camp is a week-long intensive training program designed to provide young athletes with the skills, knowledge, and inspiration they need to excel in their respective sports. Led by a team of experienced coaches and mentors, the camp offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers technical training, tactical strategies, mental conditioning, and leadership development.
